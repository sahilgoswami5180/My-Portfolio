COLLEGE WEBSITE

A college website built with HTML, CSS, PHP, and JavaScript. Students can enter their UID to access fee details and related information, improving transparency and accessibility.

Live Link

Role & Focus

Front-end and basic back-end for a college portal that allows students to quickly check fee details through a simple UID-based lookup.

(Screens show the homepage, information sections, and UID-based fee lookup.)

Project Overview

The college website is built using HTML, CSS, PHP and JavaScript. It serves as a simple portal where students can browse basic college information and use their UID to check fee-related details.

The focus is on a clean layout and a straightforward flow so that students and staff can quickly access the information they need without unnecessary complexity.
